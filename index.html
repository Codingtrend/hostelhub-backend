Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is HostelHub?

A: HostelHub is an all-in-one hostel management system for students, wardens, and administrators.

Q: How do I register as a student?

A: Click on 'Register' at the top and choose your role as Student.

Q: Can I change my room after allotment?

A: Yes, requests can be made through the student dashboard and approved by the warden.

Q: Is my data secure?

A: Yes, we prioritize user data security with encrypted access and admin controls.
